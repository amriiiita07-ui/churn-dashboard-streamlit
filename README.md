## Overview

A professional interactive analytics dashboard built to help businesses 
identify, score and act on customer churn risk before revenue is lost. 
Built with real telecom data (7,043 customers), deployed live on 
Streamlit Cloud.

---

## The Business Problem

Telecom companies lose customers monthly without knowing who is at risk 
or why. This platform provides a live, drillable, actionable view of 
churn risk so retention teams can act fast.

---

## Platform Pages

Executive Overview
- Real-time KPI cards — churn rate, MRR at risk, LTV prediction
- Retention health donut chart and weekly churn velocity trends
- Top segments ranked by churn rate with AI strategic insight

Risk Scoring
- Risk score calculated for every single customer
- Risk vs Revenue scatter matrix identifying high-value targets
- High-Risk Customer Registry for immediate intervention

Cohort Analysis
- Customer retention heatmap by cohort month
- Tenure hazard zones showing where churn peaks
- Early churn detection across Month 0-6 lifecycle

Revenue Impact
- Total annual revenue at risk calculation
- Tier-wise leakage breakdown — Critical, Growth, Legacy
- Interactive ROI calculator with campaign cost comparison
